Trip Planning

1

3 Month* Training

  • Daily Diet control
  • Daily exercise

  • Weekend parabolic flight training
  • Familiarization with space shuttle
  • Weightlessness training
  • Outdoor survival skills
  • Basic observation, math, astronomy learning

*All trainings, apart from daily habits, happen on weekends over the course of three months

2

90 Minute Earth Orbit

  • Physical and mental evaluation before launch
  • 3 person cabin crew, serving as pilots and tour guides
  • Orbit around Earth once
  • View the ocean, land, and clouds; cabin crew would introduce sites to notice on Earth
  • Take photos of Earth from over 300km up
  • Sit back and relax

Recovery

As the Earth Orbit experience is short, no special program of space recovery is necessary.
Participants would be evaluated of physical and mental health upon return to Earth. Any treatment after the orbit is included in fee and part of the experience.
