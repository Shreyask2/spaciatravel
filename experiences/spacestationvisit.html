Trip Planning

1

5 Month* Training

  • Daily Diet control
  • Daily exercise

  • Weekend parabolic flight training
  • Familiarization with space shuttle
  • Familiarization with International Space Station (ISS)
  • Learn how to live in the ISS
  • Learn tactics of space walk
  • Weightlessness training
  • Outdoor survival skills
  • Basic observation, math, astronomy learning

*All trainings, apart from daily habits, happen on weekends over the course of three months

2

Space Station Visit

  • Physical and mental evaluation before launch
  • 3 person cabin crew, serving as pilots and tour guides
  • Space station tour
  • Conduct zero-gravity experiments
  • Live, eat, and sleep in the ISS
  • Space walk outside the ISS
  • Enjoy the experience

3

Recovery

Astronauts attending the Space Station Tour would go through a period of physical therapy to get used to gravity on Earth again. The recovery period would be 1 week.

  • Physical and mental evaluation after experience
  • 2 hours daily exercise in the ISS
  • Recovery therapy including physical exercise, mental guidance, and diet control
  • Astronauts would be allowed to return home after final evaluation after recovery week
