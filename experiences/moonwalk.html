Trip Planning

1

6 Month* Training

  • Daily Diet control
  • Daily exercise

  • Weekend parabolic flight training
  • Familiarization with space shuttle
  • Familiarization with conditions on the moon
  • Learn tactics of walking on moon
  • Learn how to ride on the Lunar Roving Vehicle
  • Weightlessness training
  • Outdoor survival skills
  • Basic observation, math, astronomy learning

*All trainings, apart from daily habits, happen on weekends over the course of three months

2

Moon Walk

  • Physical and mental evaluation before launch
  • 3 person cabin crew, serving as pilots and tour guides
  • Living in space shuttle while travelling to the moon
  • Walk on the moon
  • Explore the moon riding the Lunar Roving Vehicle
  • Conduct experiments on the moon
  • Get moon souvenirs
  • Enjoy the experience

3

Recovery

Astronauts attending the Moon Walk experience would go through a period of physical therapy to get used to gravity on Earth again. The recovery period would be 2 weeks.

  • Physical and mental evaluation after experience
  • Daily exercise while travelling to and landing on the moon
  • Recovery therapy including physical exercise, mental guidance, and diet control
  • Astronauts would be allowed to return home after final evaluation after finishing their recovery weeks
