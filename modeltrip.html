Spacia Experiences Model Trip About

The Vehicles

Orbital Shuttle

Astronauts participating in the Earth Orbit and Space Station Visit experiences would be taking the Orbital Shuttle. The shuttle could accommodate up to 10 people and also deliver cargo.

Cartel 177

Astronauts participating in the Moon Walk and Mars Venture experiences would be taking Cartel 177. Cartel 177 could accommodate up to 20 people and complete planetary launches.

The Suit

Astronauts participating in all experiences would be wearing this suit while taking off, space walking, moon walking, and returning to Earth. Participants would be dressed in another astronaut suit during travelling, orbiting, or living in the ISS.
